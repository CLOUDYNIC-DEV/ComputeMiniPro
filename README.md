Compute Mini Pro: The AI Compute Pro
The Compute Mini Pro is an ultra compact, high performance carrier board designed specifically for the Rockchip RK3588 CM5 module. Built for engineers who need 6 TOPS of NPU power in their pocket, this board breaks the tradition of bulky development kits by packing industrial-grade I/O into a handheld form factor.

Engineer's Note: This entire 4-layer high-speed board was routed and finalized on an aging Inspiron N5010. It’s proof that grit and clean routing beat high-end workstations every time.

Hardware Specifications
The carrier board is engineered to unlock the full potential of the RK3588S / CM5 ecosystem:

SoC Support: Full compatibility with RK3588 CM5 modules.

PCB Architecture: 4-layer stackup with controlled impedance and length matching for LPDDR5/PCIe Gen3.

High-Speed I/O:

1x HDMI 2.1: Supports up to 8K @ 60fps output.  

1x USB 3.1 Type-C: High-speed data and power delivery.

1x Ethernet Port: Fastest transceiver for ultra-low latency networking.

Storage: MicroSD slot + eMMC support via the CM5 module.  

Power: Advanced power tree for stable delivery under heavy AI inference loads.  

Connectors: Dual high-density B2B connectors.
